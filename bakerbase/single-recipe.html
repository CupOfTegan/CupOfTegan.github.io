Ingredients

  • 100g good dark chocolate
  • 50g Amaretti biscuits
  • 100g flaked almonds, slivered
  • 175g case sugar
  • One orange zest finely grated
  • 100g butter, cut into cubes
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • cocoa powder or icing sugar for dusting

Variations

You can also make this with chocolate chips, sultanas or digestives. Just remove 2 eggs

Tips & tricks

If you don't have a food processor, you will need to use ground almonds instead of flaked, and place the Amerretti biscuits in a plastic bag and grind up with a rolling pin. Beat in a large bowl in the same order as you do with a food processor.
