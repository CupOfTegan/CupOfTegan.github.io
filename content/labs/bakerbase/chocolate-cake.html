Wicked chocolate fudge cake

Skip to recipe Skip to allergy information

Please note that this is a great recipe, but it's weird. First of all, it freaks you out putting oil and vinegar in a chocolate cake. Then you get scared because it has so much liquid and the mixture seems runny. But relax, because it works out just dandy...

Ingredients

Variations

If you don't give a damn about calories, pour a melted bar of dark chocolate over the top and let it cool (however, this will stop it being hypoallergenic!).

Method

  1. Heat oven to 190Ëšc
  2. Sift all the dry ingredients into a bowl and stir
  3. Make a well in the middle of the bowl and pour in the vinegar, then oil and water
  4. Mix until all dry ingredients are incorporated
  5. Divide mixture between two 8 inch sandwich tins
  6. Bake in oven for 35-40 minutes
  7. Let the cake cool on a wire rack
  8. Combine icing ingredients and use to sandwich the cake halves together
